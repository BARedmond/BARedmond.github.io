Work

I work at Fedex Ground as an operations manager. I have been in the position for 1 year and have worked for the company for 4 years. Education

I recieved my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from The Ohio State University. When graduating high school, the question was not "are you going to college?" but "what college are you going to?" As I proceeded through my education, I started to become increasingly disenchanted with schooling, questioning what the point of it was. If I could do it over, I would probably not go to college. I just don't think the cost was worth it for what I want to do in life, but I can't deny that extremely expensive piece of paper does still open many doors for me.
