I'm Bryce Redmond

I am a 22 year old graduate of The Ohio State University currently working as an operations manager at one of Fedex Ground's 39 American hubs. I have a Bachelor's of Science in mechanical engineering and a minor in the computer and information sciences. While my degree does open many doors, I do not place much value on it. What I do value is doing honest work that is always challenging me to improve, whether it be people skills, technical skills, or organizational skills and work that positively impacts my community directly. The work that I want to do doesn't require a $50,000 piece of paper, but rather an individual who sees work as an important aspect of life and personal growth.
